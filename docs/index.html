ANZU METU UAV TEAM

METU ANZU is an Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) project team that began its operations in 2024. Our team is composed of four main departments—Mechanical, Electronics, Software, and Organization—along with a dedicated pilot team. All members serving in these departments are students from various faculties and departments at Middle East Technical University (METU). As METU ANZU, our primary goals are to successfully represent our country and university in national and international competitions, to continuously develop ourselves in every field, and to direct our accumulated knowledge toward research and development (R&D) activities.
