A drone is only as good as the data it collects. Our focus on sensor fusion is what sets ANZU's data capture capabilities apart.

We've integrated a powerful array of sensors, but the real innovation lies in how we process their data together. This process, known as **sensor fusion**, combines information from multiple sources to create a more accurate and reliable understanding of the environment than any single sensor could provide.

Key Sensors in the Payload Bay

  • **Lidar:** For high-resolution 3D point cloud generation and terrain mapping.
  • **Hyperspectral Camera:** Captures light across many narrow bands, essential for agricultural health monitoring and mineral detection.
  • **Thermal Imager:** Used for identifying heat signatures in search and rescue or monitoring equipment health.

All sensor data is timestamped and synchronized using a custom internal board before transmission, ensuring that the final data products (like orthomosaics and digital elevation models) are accurate and geometrically consistent.
